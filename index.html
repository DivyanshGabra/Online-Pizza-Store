FAQ

How is it made?

Our pizzas are expertly crafted with precision and care. We start with hand-kneaded dough, top it with the finest ingredients, and bake it to perfection in our state-of-the-art ovens. Every step is meticulously executed to deliver a mouthwatering pizza experience.

How long does it take for delivery?

Our swift delivery service ensures your hot, delicious pizzas reach your doorstep in record time. Sit back, relax, and expect your order within 30 minutes or less. Quick, convenient, and satisfying!

Can I order for huge parties?

Planning a big party? We've got you covered! Our pizza catering service is perfect for large gatherings. From birthdays to corporate events, our delicious pizzas will satisfy everyone's cravings. Order now and let us make your party a tasty success!

Is it cooked with oil?

No, our pizzas are not cooked with oil. We use a carefully selected blend of quality ingredients and bake our pizzas to perfection in our ovens, ensuring a deliciously crispy crust without the need for additional oil. Enjoy the natural flavors!
